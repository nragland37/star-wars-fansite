ABOUT ME

My Story

Hello, I'm Nicholas!

I'm currently living in St. Louis, Missouri with my girlfriend of almost 3 years. We stay in a small but nice apartment next to the Botanical Gardens and Tower Grove park. If you're not familiar with the area, it's a beaitful part of South City with amazing events, festivals, weekly food trucks, and friendly people.

As for what I'm up to..

Well, I just graduated in Spring of 2021 from my local community college, 'STLCC - Meramec' with an associate degree. I'm also a proud member of Phi Theta Kappa which is the official national honor society for junior colleges. After leaving Meramec, I've transferred to 'Webster University' to pursue a bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

Why Star Wars?

I think it goes without saying that I’m a huge Star Wars fan, especially those written by the infamous and legendary, ‘George Lucas’. I made this website to share my love for the series and to have a place where like minded people can come to dig deeper into the Original and Prequel Star Wars saga.

Ever since I can remember, I have been a huge fan of Star Wars. The day my father pulled me out of school so we could play hooky and see Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones on opening day is something I'll never forget. I was very young, but it's something that I will always hold close to my heart.
